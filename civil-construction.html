Civil Construction

Civil engineering supports the development of vital infrastructure that sustains vibrant communities today and well into the future.

Quality, construction economy, buildability, functionality and safety are top priorities for our civil engineers working on projects of all sizes across all industry sectors. We deliver conceptual design, detailed design and value engineering services that optimise tangible project outcomes and create lasting benefits for communities.

An Integrated Approach

Whether we are creating iconic, inner-city Road Work developments to support population growth , Renewing ageing infrastructure such as the, Civil engineering is a fundamental component of many of our projects. We add real value by assisting the project team to identify and prioritise critical design elements and delivery activities. 

Our talented civil engineers are equipped with the most sophisticated design tools and constantly explore new ways to design and build using innovative materials and advanced methods of construction. We Have +15 years of indepth Experience of the U.A.E Market and well known to all the Government Authorities.

At WSP, we plan, design and manage the construction of infrastructure across the sectors of Property and Buildings, Transportation, Resources (including Industry). Delivering projects of all sizes, our experts look at complex problems from different angles and find solutions that break paradigms.
